Customer Churn Prediction

Binary classifier to detect churn risk and drive retention actions. Includes EDA, feature selection and class imbalance handling.

What I built

  • Data cleaning, outlier handling, categorical encoding (One-Hot/Ordinal).
  • Train/validation split, hyperparameter tuning for Logistic Regression and Random Forest.
  • Evaluation with ROC-AUC, precision/recall, confusion matrix and cost-sensitive thresholding.
  • Business recommendations to reduce churn (early warnings + incentives).

Tech stack
