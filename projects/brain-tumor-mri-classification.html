Brain Tumor MRI Classification

Image classification project to distinguish tumor vs. healthy MRI scans with data augmentation and a compact CNN.

What I built

  • Train/val/test split ensuring no patient leakage.
  • Augmentation pipeline (flip, rotate, zoom) to generalize better with limited data.
  • Keras/TensorFlow CNN; monitored with early stopping and learning-rate scheduling.
  • Results with accuracy/F1 and confusion matrix; sample predictions visualization.

Tech stack

Python TensorFlow/Keras NumPy Matplotlib
