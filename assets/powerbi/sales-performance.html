Sales Performance

Executive dashboard tracking Revenue, Margin and YoY growth with drill-down by product and geography.

Highlights

  • Model: Star schema (FactSales, DimDate, DimProduct, DimGeo).
  • DAX: Total Sales, Margin, YoY%, TopN.
  • Interactions: slicers, drillthrough and bookmarks.
